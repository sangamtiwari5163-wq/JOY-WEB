Established 2009

Joy Logistics Exim (India) Pvt Ltd

Founded in 2009 in India, the long-range plan of our management has focused on excellence within the Freight Forwarding market. Over the years, our services and specialized personnel have developed largely in direct response to the international transportation needs of many of the world's most prominent construction, infrastructure, and energy concerns.

Joy Logistics Exim (India) Pvt Ltd is comprehensively engaged in international forwarding, shipping, export packing and consolidation, project transportation management, custom house brokerage, marine agency, and heavy equipment carriage.

While we are heavily recognized for our expertise in project transportation, we also proudly service the shipping needs of many large and small commercial exporters and importers—primarily catering to the chemical, cement manufacturing, and heavy manufacturing fields. We bring the strong traffic expertise and rigid attention to documentation details that your premium cargo demands.
